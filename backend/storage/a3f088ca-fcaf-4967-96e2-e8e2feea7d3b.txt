
STANDARD OPERATING PROCEDURE: SOP-103
TITLE: SYSTEM AUDIT TRAILS AND DATA INTEGRITY
VERSION: 1

1.0 PURPOSE
Defines the management, protection, and review of system audit trails to prevent unauthorized modifications and ensure record traceability.

2.0 SCOPE
Applies to all computer-generated, time-stamped audit trails recording the date, time, user identity, and specific actions of system transactions.

3.0 AUDIT TRAIL MANAGEMENT
3.1 Automatic Generation: Audit trails must be automatically generated by the system and capture all record creation, modification, and deletion events.
3.2 Immutability: Audit trails must be secured against modifications, edits, or deletion by any user, including system administrators and database developers.
3.3 Regular Review: System audit trails must be reviewed by the Quality Assurance (QA) Department at least once every 30 days. Evidence of audit trail review must be documented.
3.4 Retention: Audit trails must be retained for a duration no less than the retention requirements of the electronic records they document.
