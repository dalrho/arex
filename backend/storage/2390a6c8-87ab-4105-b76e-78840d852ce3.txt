
STANDARD OPERATING PROCEDURE: SOP-104
TITLE: DOCUMENT LIFECYCLE MANAGEMENT CONTROL
VERSION: 1

1.0 PURPOSE
Governs the creation, review, approval, revision, distribution, and archival of quality system documents.

2.0 SCOPE
Applies to all standard operating procedures, design histories, and manufacturing records.

3.0 DOCUMENT LIFE-CYCLE STATES
3.1 Lifecycle States: All documents must transit through the following defined states:
  - Draft: Under active development.
  - Under Review: Circulating for stakeholder feedback.
  - Approved: Formally signed off and currently in effect.
  - Retired: Replaced by a newer version or decommissioned.
3.2 Superseding: When a document is revised, the new version supersedes the old version. The older version must be immediately flagged as 'Retired' and moved to the archive directory.
3.3 File Archival: Archived files must be stored in read-only folders with modification rights disabled to prevent deletion or overwriting.
